Tab Operations (work on all tabs)

This is a special category of Chrome extension shortcuts. They are different in that they also work on tabs that do not have any web content loaded. For example: a new tab. Additionally, they also work on tabs that have Units disabled.

To change these, go to the Chrome extensions page . Scroll to the bottom, and click on "Keyboard Shortcuts" for editing options.
