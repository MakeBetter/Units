Thanks for installing the Units extension. Here are some tips to get you quickly started...

Navigate between units

  1. Visit one of: Google Search, Facebook, Twitter or Stack Overflow *
  2. Notice that the first main unit on the page is selected. Press j and k to move between the units.
  3. In most cases, the most important link in the unit is already selected for you. Press o to open it in a new tab or enter to open in the the same tab.
  4. Use tab to move focus to the next link in the unit/page.

Note: For pages that don't have any units defined, j and k will simply smooth scroll the page.

* A list of all the supported websites here.

Invoke unit based shortcuts

Units allows you to have consistent shortcuts across websites that work on the selected unit. For example, press u to upvote, s to share, m to 'see more', c to comment on a post on Facebook, Quora, Hacker News or a StackExchange site.

Bring up Units Help to see all the shortcuts defined for the page that you are on.

Read with more focus

Press + to increase the spotlight effect on the selected unit and - to decrease it.

Filter units

This is a smart "find" feature - keep only those units on the page that match your search term. Try it out, you'll especially like it on Facebook. Press alt + f to bring up the search UI.

Disable Units

  • To temporarily disable Units on any page, press ,
  • To permanently disable Units on a site: Click on the Units extension icon in the browser and disable from there.

Configure

Units is highly customizable. Head over to the Options page of the extension to change settings and shortcuts.

Units Help

The Help dialog lists all the shortcuts that are valid for a specific page. Click on the extension icon to get to Help, or press alt + / to bring up the dialog.
